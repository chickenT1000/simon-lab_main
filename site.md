# Simon-Lab

Simon-Lab provides computational engineering for chemical projects. The company focuses on code-native workflows that make engineering projects traceable, verifiable, auditable, and ready for computational tooling and AI-assisted review.

## Website
- Canonical URL: https://simon-lab.org/
- Languages: English, Polish, German

## Services and positioning
- Computational engineering for chemical projects
- Project-as-Code methodology
- Structured engineering data for validation and reuse
- AI-ready engineering workflows

## Applications
### LabelHash
- Detail page: https://simon-lab.org/apps/labelhash.html
- Product URL: https://label.simon-lab.org
- Purpose: ISO 20560-compliant label generation for pipelines, tanks, reactors, and vessels

### ProcessView
- Detail page: https://simon-lab.org/apps/process-view.html
- Purpose: web-based P&ID visualization with embedded physics simulation for chemical plant design and control
